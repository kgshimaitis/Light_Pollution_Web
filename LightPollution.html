This website is a resource to learn about Light Pollution, how it affects our daily lives in New York City, and what we can do to help.

What is Light Pollution?

There are countless environmental crises occuring in the world, most of them occuring because of human pollution. Most people have heard about or witnessed major types of pollution such as water pollution in oceans and lakes, or air pollution in major cities, but many people are unaware that light can also be polluted.

Light Pollution is the inappropriate or excessive use of artificial light that can have serious environmental consequences for humans, wildlife, and our climate.
